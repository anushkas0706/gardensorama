About Us

What we do ?

Gardensorama started out quite a different company then we are now and through many years of hard work, building relationships & and sourcing the very best for our customers we could not be prouder of our small family run business as it stands today. We offer Landscaping, Garden Design, Garden, Grounds & Property Maintenance, Timber Structures & Water Garden Services. ​ Gardensorama works closely with our customers on a face-to-face basis to ensure all your personal touches are included and to guarantee quality & satisfaction throughout. Based on lot of experiences, we are surrounded by inspiration. ​ Throughout the years we have improved our methods & most importantly the quality of work through constantly striving to find the best products throughout India & in many cases, the world. Importing from South America, UK & France for our Stone & Timber Products to always guarantee the very best. We have also come to supply some of our own materials within the company as the service and quality we found from outsourcing was not good enough to meet our standards. Serving both Domestic & Commercial Clients. ​
